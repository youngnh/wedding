Our honeymoon

Nate's parents have made the very generous gift of sending their son and future daughter-in-law to Sandals Emerald Bay in the Bahamas.

We'll be leaving the Monday after the wedding, June 14th, to fly to Miami. From there we'll fly into Aruba where we'll be met by a prop plane to take us to the resort as it is on a island too small to hold a full size runway. We will be staying in the Emerald Bay Village, in a Honeymoon Luxury suite, just a stroll off the crystal clear waters of the Caribbean.

We couldn't be more excited about our honeymoon. We both wanted a lazy, no worries escape on a beach somewhere in a sunny climate with plenty of sand, water and cool breezes rustling through the palm trees. We'll be in for all that and more after the wedding.
